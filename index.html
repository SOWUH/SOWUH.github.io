Here's How The Magic Happens


I love my job. Just like Yor loves hers!

So, you're thinking about a commission? Awesome! Let's do this! How? Here is how:
1. You hit me up. We'll chat about your idea, figure out the details, and agree on the price and what to expect.
2. Once we're set, I'll need the full payment upfront to get your spot in my queue. This lets me dive into the art without any boring admin stuff getting in the way.
3. When it's your turn, I'll whip up a sketch and send it over. This is your moment! Any changes you want are welcome and free at this stage, so don't be shy.
4. ??????
5. After you give the sketch a thumbs-up, I'll retreat into my art cave to finish the piece. Then, I’ll send the finished picture to you and post it for the world to see.


A few other things to keep in mind:

No secrets! I don't do private commissions. I pour my heart into this work and I want to be able to share it. That means anything I create for you, I'll be posting online.

It's ours. The final artwork belongs to both of us, 50/50. You can do whatever you want with it, and so can I. Fair's fair!
