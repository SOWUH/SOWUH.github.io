What can affect base price?

My main goal with pricing is to make picture you want for the best price I can.
But the complexity of drawing is a very subjective concept. In some cases, a seemingly simple picture can take a lot of time and effort and vice versa. It's hard to predict so in most cases base price will be best option.

Also you can count on a discount if you want me to draw a fanart for some popular game/series/etc.

If you want a really simple picture or something epic/extremely detailed — it could affect the cost of my work both ways. We will discuss it and will look at all possible options.
Pictures for printing or very high resolution require additional attention and could cost more.
I require no additional payment for two versions of one picture if differences are small. If you want more versions or/and pics vary much — additional payment starts from $10.

How does it work?

SOWUH.art@gmail.com
Mail me description of picture you want. Find some references — pics that describe your idea better than thousand words. I want them badly.
I'll ask you few questions to make sure I did get you right. After that I'll tell you how much it costs, how much time will it take (usually about a month) and send you PayPal invoice. I work only with fullprice prepay.
Your art will be in line at the moment you pay.

I'll start developing sketch as soon as your turn comes. After it's ready I'll send sketch to you for approval.
You welcome to make some edits on this stage before you give me green light to start making final picture.
When art is ready I'll send it to you and publish it.

We share copyrights for the final picture 50/50. That means that we both can do whatever we want with it. I can mention you or keep your identity secret — as you wish.
